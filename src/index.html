Our Story

Our paths crossed in September 2020, leading us into a profound love story, woven into the fabric of the universe's grand design. Fueled by passion and a commitment to spreading love within the LGBTQ+ community, we began our transformative journey, launching a YouTube channel just one month later.

Since marrying in August 2022 and embracing Ohio as our home, our shared wanderlust has taken us on incredible adventures worldwide, deepening our bond and creating cherished memories.

Today, our hearts are filled with joy as we curate must-haves for our home, venture into the wonders of foreign lands, and cherish the boundless growth we experience together.
